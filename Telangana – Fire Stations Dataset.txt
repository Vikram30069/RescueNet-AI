Quality Control Summary
• Records found: ~150 fire stations across Telangana (names from official “Know Your Fire Station” list
and Station Officers directory).
• Verified records: All phone numbers above are from the official Telangana Fire Services directory
【20†L85-L92】【24†L95-L103】 (HIGH confidence). Addresses are from business listing sites (not
official), so marked UNVERIFIED (LOW confidence).
• District coverage: Collected stations in Hyderabad/Secunderabad (22 listed), Sangareddy
(Patancheru, Sangareddy, Sadasivpet, Zaheerabad, etc.), Warangal (Warangal, Narsampet, etc.),
Nizamabad district (Nizamabad City, Bodhan, etc.), Karimnagar (Karimnagar, Huzurabad, etc.),
Khammam (Khammam, Sathupally, etc.), and others. Coverage primarily covers priority cities above.
• Missing districts: Some rural/outpost stations have limited info (e.g., district outposts). No data
found for a few remote posts or newer stations.
• Recommended sources: Future updates should consult the Telangana Fire Department for official
address listings, district emergency contact lists, or field verification. Cross-reference with local
government/public safety portals or RTI queries to the Fire Services may yield exact locations and
alternate numbers.
